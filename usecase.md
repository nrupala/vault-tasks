# 🚀 Vault Tasks: Professional Use Cases

Vault Tasks isn't just a simple checklist; it's a zero-trust productivity engine for high-stakes environments.

### 1. ⚖️ Legal & Investigative Case Tracking
* **Scenario**: Tracking sensitive discovery items, witness interviews, and evidence deadlines.
* **Why Vault Tasks**: Data is encrypted locally (AES-256). Even if a computer is seized, the vault remains inaccessible without the master password. No cloud leakage to opposing counsel.

### 2. 🧪 Scientific & Medical Research
* **Scenario**: Tracking clinical trial phases, patient observation checklists, or sensitive IP development.
* **Why Vault Tasks**: Ensure HIPAA-adjacent privacy for research notes and task lists. No accidental exposure through corporate cloud providers.

### 3. 🛡️ Cybersecurity Operations (Blue/Red Team)
* **Scenario**: Managing active compromise remediation steps or penetration testing checklists.
* **Why Vault Tasks**: Keep operationally sensitive procedures off of centralized project management tools that could be compromised themselves.

### 4. 🏥 Personal Health & Symptom Tracking
* **Scenario**: Managing complex chronic illness treatment plans or sensitive mental health routines.
* **Why Vault Tasks**: Keep your health journey 100% private. No data mining by insurance companies or ad networks.
